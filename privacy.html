Privacy Policy

Internal Software Integrations — Grow Space, LLC
Last updated: April 21, 2026

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Grow Space, LLC (a Wisconsin limited liability company operating a hydroponic vertical farm in Kenosha, Wisconsin) handles data in connection with internal software integrations between our accounting platform (QuickBooks Online) and our operations and business intelligence systems.

This policy applies only to internal-use integrations. These integrations are not distributed publicly, are not offered as a commercial product, and collect no data from the general public.

What We Access

Through these integrations, Grow Space, LLC accesses data belonging to Grow Space, LLC, including:

  • Accounting records stored in our QuickBooks Online company
  • Chart of accounts, customers, vendors, items, and transactions within that company
  • Farm operations records (tasks, harvests, inventory, trays) stored in our Compass and FarmIQ platforms
  • Business intelligence aggregates derived from the above

No personal data is collected from any party outside Grow Space, LLC.

Who Has Access

Only authorized members and officers of Grow Space, LLC have access to the integrations. Access is gated by Microsoft Entra ID single sign-on via Cloudflare Access, restricted to approved email addresses.

How Data Is Used

Data accessed through these integrations is used solely to:

  • Manage the books and financial records of Grow Space, LLC
  • Automate accounting entries generated by farm operations
  • Produce internal reports for the business

Data is not sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties, except:

  • As necessary to operate the integrations
  • As required by law, court order, or lawful subpoena

Data Storage

  • Accounting data remains within Intuit's QuickBooks Online infrastructure
  • Integration tokens and session state are stored in Cloudflare's infrastructure (Workers KV and Durable Objects)
  • Operations data is stored in Supabase project infrastructure under our control
  • No data is retained outside these systems

Security

We use standard security practices including OAuth 2.0 authentication, encrypted storage of credentials, single sign-on via Microsoft Entra ID, and access controls via Cloudflare Access. Tokens are rotated automatically.

Third-Party Processors

  • Intuit, Inc. — hosts the QuickBooks Online accounting data
  • Microsoft Corporation — provides identity verification via Entra ID
  • Cloudflare, Inc. — hosts the integration worker and mediates authentication
  • Anthropic PBC — operates Claude, which acts as the AI interface to the integration when used by authorized personnel

Each of these parties operates under their own privacy practices.

Data Retention

  • Access and refresh tokens are retained only as long as the integration is actively connected
  • Upon disconnection, tokens are revoked and purged
  • Accounting data retention is governed by QuickBooks Online's policies and applicable tax and accounting law

Changes

Grow Space, LLC may update this policy at any time. The current version is always available at this URL.
